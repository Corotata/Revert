Showcases Auto Layout alignments, pinning and layout margins interacting. The widths of all squares have have been constrained to be equal to the width of the centre square.

A UISlider is displayed on devices running iOS 8 or higher. The slider's value is bound to the centre square's layoutMargins attribute.

Try updating the constant value of the centre view's width constraint, and see the changes being reflected in Reveal and on your device or simulator.
