A collection of different CALayer subclasses:

  • CATextLayer
  • CAEmitterLayer
  • CAShapeLayer
  • CAScrollLayer
  • CAGradientLayer
  • CAReplicatorLayer
  • CATiledLayer
  • CAEAGLayer

This example demonstrates Reveal's support for different CALayer subclasses.

In Reveal try selecting the CAShapeLayerView and then altering the Line, Stroke and Fill attributes on the Layer inspector to see how they alter the rendering of the circle.
